### What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents the convergence of artificial intelligence with the physical world. Unlike traditional AI that operates purely in digital environments, Physical AI systems:

- **Understand Physics**: Comprehend gravity, friction, collisions, and other physical laws
- **Perceive Reality**: Use sensors (cameras, LiDAR, IMUs) to understand their environment
- **Manipulate Objects**: Interact with the physical world through actuators and end-effectors
- **Navigate Space**: Plan paths, avoid obstacles, and move through 3D environments
- **Learn from Experience**: Improve performance through interaction with the real world

## ü§ñ Why Humanoid Robotics?

Humanoid robots are poised to excel in our human-centered world because they:

- **Share Our Form**: Navigate stairs, doorways, furniture designed for humans
- **Use Human Tools**: Operate equipment built for human hands
- **Learn from Human Data**: Leverage abundant training data from demonstrations
- **Communicate Naturally**: Interact through gestures and social cues
- **Adapt to Environments**: Thrive in spaces designed for human ergonomics

This represents a transition from AI models confined to digital environments to **embodied intelligence** in physical space.
